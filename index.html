Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Welcome to OmniOracle. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how our mobile application handles data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

OmniOracle does not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

To support our free service, we use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may automatically collect and process certain data, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Android Advertising ID.
  • App Interaction: Information about how you interact with ads within the app.

2. GDPR Compliance (EEA Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we process your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent: We request your explicit consent before displaying personalized ads.
  • Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, or request deletion of data held by our advertising partners.
  • International Transfers: Data may be processed in countries outside the EEA, primarily the United States, under Google's safety frameworks.

3. US State Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California or other US states with specific privacy laws:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information is collected and shared.
  • Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. You can do this via the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link provided in the app or through your device's Google settings.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not deny service or provide a different quality of service if you exercise your privacy rights.

4. Use of Information

Data collected via AdMob is used for:

  • Providing and optimizing ad delivery.
  • Personalizing ad content (where consent is given).
  • Analyzing app performance and preventing ad fraud.

5. Children's Privacy

OmniOracle is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Choices & Opt-Out

You can limit ad tracking at any time:

  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID.
  • Web: Manage your preferences at Google My Ad Center.
